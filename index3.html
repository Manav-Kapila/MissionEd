Let's Get MissionEd

At MISSIONED, we believe every child has the right to high-quality
educational resources and experiences. We exist to ensure ANY STUDENT,
ANYWHERE, ANYTIME has an equal and equitable opportunity for success.

Vision, Mission, Purpose and Values

MISSIONED helps educators and families to facilitate distance learning opportunities. Our goal is to make distance
learning as credible as traditional learning.

1.

Free and paid resources that are turn-key
ready for use with students.

2.

Tutorials on how to design and deliver
asynchronous learning

3.

Guidance on how to support remote
learners

4.

Resources to foster a healthy distance
learning environment

5.

K-12 digital lessons aligned to national
and state standards
